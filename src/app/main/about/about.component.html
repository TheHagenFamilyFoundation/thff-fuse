About

The Hagen Family Foundation (THFF) is a private family foundation established in 1999 that provides financial grants to not-for-profit organizations.

THFF favors creative and innovative proposals in the areas of:

  • The Arts
  • Education
  • Environment
  • Religion
  • Social Services

THFF does not consider contributions to annual drives, capital campaigns, research, or the support of on-going programs. THFF supports organizations nationwide, with special interest in Colorado, Michigan and Florida. Grants range from $1,000 to $20,000.
